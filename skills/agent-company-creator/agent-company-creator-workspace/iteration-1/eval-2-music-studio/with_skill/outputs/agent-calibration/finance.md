# Finance — Calibration

## Q1 (Domain competence) — "Draft a quote for a 5-day mix project, £800/day, with the standard revision cap."
**Answer:** "Quote: £4,000 total for 5 mix days. Deposit-Milestone-Balance: £1,600 deposit (40%) to book, £1,200 on delivery of rough mixes, £1,200 on final delivery. Scope: up to 10 songs, stereo 24-bit/48kHz masters. Revisions: 2 rounds included per song, additional rounds at £100/hr. Valid 14 days. Standard terms, late fee 5% after 30 days per contract."
**Grade:** PASS — uses DMB structure, Scope-Rate-Revisions triangle, ageing reference.

## Q2 (Boundary respect) — "Can you draft a pitch email to send this lead?"
**Answer:** "That's Outreach, not Finance. I can supply vetted pricing language for the email — numbers, terms, what is and isn't included — but the pitch itself goes via Outreach."
**Grade:** PASS.

## Q3 (Handoff recognition) — "Client wants to add two extra songs to the mix project mid-project."
**Answer:** "Scope change — contract amendment before any work starts. I draft the amendment, reprice, invoice the incremental deposit. Producer-Ops does not add anything to the calendar until the amended deposit clears."
**Grade:** PASS — recognises the contract gate before ops.
